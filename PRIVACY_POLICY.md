# Privacy Policy — Promises

**Effective date: June 12, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how the **Promises** app ("the app", "we", "our")
handles your information. The short version: **the app does not collect,
transmit, or share any personal data.**

> **Promises works entirely offline.** It has no internet permission and makes
> no network connections. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising,
> and no third-party tracking of any kind.

## Information we collect

We do **not** collect, store on our servers, or transmit any personal or
sensitive information. The app has no user accounts and never asks for your
name, email, location, contacts, or any other personal details.

## Information stored on your device

The app saves a small amount of data **locally on your device only**, in the
app's private storage. This never leaves your device and is not accessible to us:

- **Favorite verses** — the verses you mark with the heart, so you can find them again.
- **Reminder preferences** — whether the daily verse notification is on, and the time you chose for it.

This data is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its storage.

## Permissions the app uses

- **Notifications** — used only to show the optional daily verse reminder. The
  reminder is off until you turn it on, and you can turn it off at any time in
  Settings or in your device settings.
- **Run after restart (boot completed)** — used only to re-schedule your daily
  reminder after your device restarts, so the reminder keeps working. No data is
  read or sent.

## Sharing a verse

When you tap Share, the app uses your device's standard Android share sheet and
you choose where to send the verse text. We do not select the recipient, and we
do not receive or store anything you share. Any app you share to is governed by
that app's own privacy policy.

## Device backup

Android may include the app's local preferences (your favorites and reminder
time) in your own device's backup if you have Android/Google backup enabled.
That backup is controlled by you and by Google, under
[Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy), not by us.

## Children's privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information from
anyone, including children.

## Third parties

The app contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party
services that would receive your data.

## Scripture text

Bible passages in the app are from the World English Bible (WEB), which is in
the public domain. This is provided for context and is unrelated to data
collection.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the effective date
above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
